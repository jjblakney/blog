What is Time?

What is time? It may sound like a silly question because it is such in important part of our lives. Time may actually not be what you think it is. Many great minds through history have pondered time. From great philosophers to scientist many people have given their input. Is time real or is it simply an illusion? Take a brief journey with me to discuss what time is.

"Time is what we want most, but what we use worst"(William Penn)
